Hick's Law

Humble Bundle - Website

This website is for people interested in getting video games for a great deal. When you first open it up it shows you one of their best deals along with a timer to add emphasis on purchasing now.

This displays Hick's Law because of the simple information presented along with having the customer want to make a decision on purchasing or not.
